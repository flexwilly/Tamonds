About Us

Tamods Limited is a registered limited company in Kenya that began its business operation in 2021 to bridging the gap on access of medical Equipment's and consumables. Tamods Limited is focusing exclusively in high quality and cost-effective medical Equipment's and consumables.

We are advancing on a tremendous pace and with involvement of skilled and experienced people working in the organization. Tamods Limited is currently partnering both locally and internationally to ensure the Organization Vision and mission are achieved.
