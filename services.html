Products and Services

Tamods Limited Supply carries medical supply and equipment categories that support or treat numerous acute or ongoing/chronic medical conditions with comprehensive selection of medical supplies and equipment.

With over 100 products and direct accounts with key manufacturers and exclusive distributors around the globe, we can ensure a varied selection of products at reasonable prices, as well as the ability to easily source what we don’t have.

At Tamods, we know how important it is to get medical products, fast. We keep all high volume products in stock so that we can deliver to our customers across East, South & Central Africa within two business days. Unlike other medical supply companies, we offer many products in smaller units of measure to support community facilities.
